Hi, I'm Yamini Sawant

The heart and hands behind Rangsaj Arts

Every piece starts with a story...

I started Rangsaj Arts to give life to the beauty I see in emotions, traditions, and celebrations. From handcrafted wedding invites to personalized baby boards, I believe in capturing meaning — not just making pretty things.

My work is rooted in love, inspired by culture, and touched by the personal moments you share with me. Rangsaj isn’t just a name — it’s a feeling, a splash of color in life’s biggest milestones.

“My art is a reflection of emotions — carefully painted with heritage, color and soul.” – Yamini Sawant
